The Festival

Experience the biggest Afrobeats celebration in North America! AfroMusic Fest 2025 brings together the hottest artists, authentic African cuisine, vibrant culture, and non-stop dancing under the Edmonton sky.


Headlined by international superstar DAVIDO, this two-day festival showcases the best of Afrobeats, Amapiano, and contemporary African music. Join thousands of music lovers for an unforgettable weekend of rhythm, culture, and pure joy.

Featured Artists

DAVIDO

Nigerian superstar bringing hits like "Fall", "If", and "Fem" to Edmonton. The OBO himself headlines this incredible festival experience.

Amapiano Legends

South Africa's finest Amapiano DJs and producers delivering those signature log drum beats and soulful melodies that move the crowd.

Rising Stars

Discover the next generation of African music talent, from Afro-fusion to contemporary highlife and everything in between.

Local Favourites

Edmonton's own African music ambassadors showcasing the vibrant local scene and representing the diaspora community.
